From Me to You

Memory holds impactful visuals attached to the feelings of life. The angle and curve of lips, the story told through the eyes targeting truth. The fear of remembrance leaving your body or never leaving the attachment to your soul. Granting emotion through the impact of one moment. Holding that sensation for so many to know, to mark that in their memory for them to unfold. Split off of mixed expressions, but always privileged to bring to the eyes of the viewer.
